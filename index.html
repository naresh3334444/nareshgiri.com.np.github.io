Foreign Policy

Securing security interest, economic interest and value interest

Foreign policy is a nation-state's strategic framework to secure its security, economic, and value interests globally. It involves diplomatic, economic, military, and cultural actions to enhance security through alliances, deterrence, and conflict resolution, while advancing economic prosperity via trade, investment, and resource management. Additionally, it upholds core values like democracy and human rights through international cooperation and confronting transnational challenges, serving as a dynamic instrument for navigating global complexities and ensuring national well-being.
