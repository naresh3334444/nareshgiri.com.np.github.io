Protection of Security, Economic and Value Interests

In foreign policy, safeguarding security entails diplomatic efforts to for survival of the country in anarchic international world. Economic interests encompass development cooperation, foreign trade, FDI, foreign employment and tourism. Value interests influence ideology, cultural diplomacy and mobilization of diaspora for enhancing national prestige in international arena.
